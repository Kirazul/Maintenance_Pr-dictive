Feature Guide
Sensors (Raw) Engineered (New) Cuts & Flags
Raw Sensors

Standard Input Features

These are the base sensor readings captured directly from the hardware.

Feature Description Physical Context
air_temp_k Air Temperature Ambient environmental heat surrounding the machine.
process_temp_k Process Temperature Internal heat generated during the machining process.
rotational_speed_rpm Rotational Speed The velocity of the spindle/motor in RPM.
torque_nm Torque The rotational force applied by the machine.
tool_wear_min Tool Wear Cumulative duration the tool has been in active use.
Engineered

Derived Technical Indicators

These features are calculated to capture non-linear relationships between sensors.

Feature Formula Signal Goal
temp_delta_k Process - Air Temp Measures thermal spread/heat dissipation efficiency.
power_proxy Speed * Torque Estimates total energy output/mechanical effort.
wear_power_ratio Wear / Power Identifies inefficiency (high wear for low power).
torque_speed_ratio Torque / Speed Detects mechanical imbalance or clogging risks.
thermal_load Delta T * Torque Captures combined thermal and physical stress.
wear_temp_stress Wear * Delta T Accumulated damage under high thermal load.
Policy Logic

Binarization & Risk Bands

Turning continuous values into discrete states for cleaner decision making.

Feature Logic (Cut) Operation Rationale
high_torque_flag Torque >= 55 Nm Marks the mechanical safe-operating limit.
low_speed_flag Speed <= 1400 RPM Identifies stalling zones where heat builds up.
wear_risk_band Binned [0, 50, 120, 180+] Classifies tool life into action-oriented zones.
